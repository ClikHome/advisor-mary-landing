Turn your visitors into renters with Apartment Ocean Chatbot

Chatbots have been used by many large corporations to provide automated and human-like communication with customers.

They are normally seen as pop up chat sections on the bottom of a webpage, and it’s about time they found their way onto real estate sites. Chatbots are programmed to respond to a variety of questions that an average person would ask. Like Siri, they respond uniquely to what is said to them, and can link users to wide databases of information. The applications of this technology for real estate are practically limitless.

A potential renter could go to a chatbot at any hour of the day and ask things like, “Show me 3 bedroom apartments in Chicago,” or “Tell me all the available apartments on 14th St. for under $5,000 a month.” The chatbot could even pull up images or maps for the user to see, all while simulating the experience of instant messaging with another person.
